The SME Play

Capturing Entrepreneurs Before They Scale

GrowKwik’s primary audience is budding D2C entrepreneurs — people just starting their first online brand, learning what checkout conversion means. This is the same SME segment where Razorpay has historically been the default. GoKwik intercepts these founders before they grow large enough to have a vendor conversation, embedding GoKwik as the trusted default from day one. By the time these businesses scale, the switching cost is high and loyalty is established.
