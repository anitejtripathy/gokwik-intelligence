EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Threat in One Paragraph

GoKwik has commissioned a structured, full-funnel content operation through the creator agency GrowKwik ("India's #1 D2C channel"), spanning 97 dedicated feature-push posts and 474 thought-leadership posts across 600 total videos — generating an estimated 31.5 million total views. The content is designed to intercept D2C founders before they enter any vendor consideration process, educating them on checkout friction and COD risk using GoKwik-framed narratives, then converting problem-aware viewers into warm app install leads via a keyword comment-to-DM funnel with UTM-tracked Shopify App Store links. By the time a founder begins evaluating solutions, GoKwik's framing is already the reference frame. Magic Checkout competes at the product evaluation stage. GoKwik is winning the stage before that.

Why This Matters for Razorpay

CRITICAL
Upstream pre-emption of the consideration window.

GoKwik reaches D2C founders at the problem-awareness stage — before they know they need an OTP checkout or COD management tool. The 474 thought-leadership posts establish GoKwik's vocabulary for the problem. By the time a founder evaluates solutions, GoKwik's framing is the reference frame.

HIGH
Category ownership in OTP checkout and COD.

The two capabilities GoKwik most explicitly promotes — single OTP checkout and COD management — are Magic Checkout's core value propositions. Two videos explicitly use fear-based COD crisis narratives ("Shopify is shutting down its COD app," "COD is dying in India") to manufacture urgency with GoKwik as the only resolution.

HIGH
No equivalent Razorpay-owned media presence.

GoKwik has a 31.5M-view, compounding content asset library educating its target segment. Razorpay has no identified equivalent targeting D2C founders at the problem-awareness stage. This is a structural gap — it cannot be closed with a single campaign push.

MEDIUM
Merchant pricing sensitivity risk.

Comments on high-engagement D2C content surface repeated pricing objections ("too costly," "not for everyone"). If Magic Checkout's pricing is perceived as premium relative to GoKwik's, and GoKwik occupies the trusted educator position, Razorpay risks being framed as the expensive alternative in a segment where trust was built elsewhere.

Three Strategic Options

01
Build Competing Owned Media

✓ Builds a durable, compounding asset GoKwik cannot replicate ✗ High investment, long time-to-impact, execution not guaranteed
02
Commission a Creator Partnership
⚡ Weeks to launch

Identify an existing D2C education channel with comparable or larger audience reach and commission structured Magic Checkout integrations using GrowKwik's own playbook — narrative case studies, keyword comment-to-DM funnels, UTM-tracked install links.

✓ Fastest response, leverages existing audiences ✗ Creator dependency, less control, perception risk of following
03
Dominate the Decision Stage
⏱ Near-term implementable

Accept GoKwik owns the awareness layer. Invest in dominating evaluation and decision stages — where a GoKwik-aware founder then encounters Razorpay through direct sales, a superior free trial, or a checkout conversion benchmark comparison tool.

✓ Higher ROI if Magic Checkout conversion data is genuinely superior ✗ Cedes education layer to GoKwik permanently
